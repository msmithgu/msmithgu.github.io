Twisty Cube Lesson (3x3x3)

Notation

 
U
Up
D
Down
L
Left
R
Right
F
Front
B
Back

Twist clockwise into the cube one quarter turn (90 degrees).

'
"prime" indicates reverse of move
2
"twice" or a half turn (180 degrees)

Examples

F2 reads "F two" and means to rotate the Front face a half turn or 180 degrees.

U reads "U" and means screw the Up face a quarter turn as if into the cube.

B reads "B" and means screw the Back face a quarter turn as if into the cube.

U' reads "U prime" and means unscrew the Up face a quarter turn as if out of the cube.

D' reads "D prime" and means unscrew Down face a quarter turn as if out of the cube.
